Gracia Redentora
An organization dedicated to serve those in need
Our goal is to grow and establish Gracia Redentora not only in Mexico, but in all Latin America according to god's will

About Us
We are currently working with people in the Greater Los Angeles area of California, Baja California, Ensenada, and neighboring areas.
We have projects of food, clothing and toys for orphanages, as well as establishing senior centers for people who don’t have family or a place to live.
